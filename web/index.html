How It Works

MediaPipe HandLandmarker detects 21 key points of your hand. JavaScript calculates angles and distances to recognize gestures, executing actions instantly.

Virtual Laser Pointer

Make a Victory Sign (Peace gesture ✌️) for 1.5 seconds to toggle the virtual laser pointer. Once active, move your hand and the laser dot will follow your index finger!

✌️ Hold Victory Sign to toggle. Move hand to point.
